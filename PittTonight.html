Human trafficking is a critical and rampant issue that over 21 million victims are coerced into around the world. Anyone can become a victim of human trafficking, especially those who are in a state of vulnerability. Human trafficking takes occurs in many forms such as working for little or no pay, forced prostitution and debt bondage. More often than less, we don't recognize these events taking place all around us. Our campaign, #iGottaFeeling is targeted to establish a platform for educating the younger generations on the alerting signs of human trafficking and how to prevent being put in a vulnerable situation. We want to be a resourceful outlet in guiding a movement with your help towards a safer and more loving world. Let's Stop This Traffic!


Red Flags:

  • Works excessively/unusual hours
  • Unable to pay off a large debt they owe
  • Running away from home
  • Showing states of vulnerability
  • Abnormal Behaviour: anxious, depressed, paraniod, nervous, submissive, tense
  • Shows signs of physical or sexual abuse
  • Has few or no personal belongings
  • Scared or unable to speak for themselves
  • Lack of knowledge on their whereabouts
  • Avoids eye contact
  • Has this person stopped attending school or their job?
  • Is the person being escorted around?
  • Location whereabouts: are they seen going into secluded and suspicious areas
  • Does not have ID on them
  • Loss of a sense of time


Listed above are some of the many red flags that may indicate signs of human trafficking. These are alerting signs that you should reach out for help to local authorities, a crisis hotline, or even a friend. Nobody should be left to deal with this alone.
